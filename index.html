Frequently Asked Questions

Is Finovo really 100% free forever?

expand_more
Yes, absolutely. We designed Finovo as a community-first tool. There are no subscriptions, no hidden charges, and no feature-locking. All current and future core updates will remain free.

Does it support offline management?

expand_more
Finovo is built to work completely offline. Your data resides on your device, ensuring maximum privacy and the ability to manage finances even without an internet connection.

Can I manage both personal and business accounts?

expand_more
Yes! Our hybrid architecture allows you to toggle between personal expense tracking and the Udhaar Book (Party Ledger) within the same app, keeping your finances organized but distinct.
